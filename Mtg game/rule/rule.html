Ruling

Everycard will be evaluated carefully with this table as a ruler

Level Description
S 10 Level 10:
Absolutely strong, must be included in every deck (only reason for not putting in your deck is because it is too strong) and consider one of the strongest card in commander.
9 Level 9
Really strong, should be included in every deck. This card will have 1 or 2 downside scenarios.
A 8 Level 8:
Strong, need be included in every deck. This card will have about 3 or 4 downside scenarios.
7 Level 7:
Kinda strong, can be included in every deck. The downside scenarios can not put this card to the unplayable state.
B 6 Level 6:
Kinda Bad, should be consider as filler for the rest of your deck if there is any empty spot.
5 Level 5:
Bad, but this will be good in one or two scenarios or the synergy with the commander is good.
C 4 Level 4:
Really bad, this type of card will be considering as jank but have to use it because a good synergy with commander and there is no alternative selection
3 Level 3
Absolutely bad, same as level 4 but there are better alternative selection
D 2 Level 2:
Somewhat jank, can make your deck functionality go down but with some miracle "Magic land" upside
1 Level 1:
Jank, this card can make your deck functionality go down.

The rules above are my personal rules as well as many suggestion from MTGGoldfish and EDHREC. For updating or feedback please go to Feedback page
